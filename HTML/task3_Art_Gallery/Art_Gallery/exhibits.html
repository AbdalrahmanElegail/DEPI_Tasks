Exhibits



Abstract Landscapes

Left Artwork: Features swirling patterns resembling aerial views of terrain or water formations. Earthy tones dominate, giving a sense of natural textures.

Middle Artwork: Highlights a vivid aerial perspective of land and water, with bright turquoise and brown hues creating a contrast between the natural and artificial elements.

Right Artwork: Displays a close-up abstraction of river deltas or glacial patterns, with bold blue and black shades. The intricate textures suggest the flow and erosion of natural elements.

Artist: Lila Karim



Agricultural Terraces

Bird's-eye view of layered terraces, likely rice fields, with reflective water surfaces interspersed with greenery. The symmetry and intricate patterns create a sense of rhythm and balance.

Artist: Sophia Rivers



Monochromatic Forests

Left Artwork: A small grayscale painting depicting a forest floor with fallen logs and branches.

Right Triptych: Three interconnected panels feature a larger composition of uprooted trees and forest debris. The texture and shading convey destruction and renewal in a natural setting.

Artist: Ethan Morales
