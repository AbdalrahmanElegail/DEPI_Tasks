Our Artists




Ethan Morales

Bio: Ethan Morales is an experimental multimedia artist from Mexico City. His work incorporates digital art, photography, and sculpture, often reflecting themes of identity, cultural heritage, and urbanization. Ethan holds a degree in Fine Arts from the Universidad Nacional Autónoma de México and has exhibited his work internationally.

Description: Ethan's latest series, “Fragments of a City,” combines digital prints with sculptural installations to depict the evolution of urban landscapes. His art delves into the collision between tradition and modernity, showcasing intricate textures and vibrant contrasts. Each piece tells a story of resilience and transformation, rooted in the bustling heart of the metropolis.


Sophia Rivers

Bio: Sophia Rivers is a contemporary artist specializing in abstract landscapes. Based in Amsterdam, her work captures the essence of nature through vivid color palettes and bold brushstrokes. Her inspiration stems from her travels across the Alps, the Sahara Desert, and the Amazon rainforest.

Description: Sophia's works explore the interplay of light, color, and form in nature, creating a sense of movement and energy. Her recent collection, “Ethereal Horizons,” features paintings that blur the line between reality and imagination. Her pieces evoke a sense of wonder and introspection, inviting viewers to experience the raw beauty of the world through her eyes.


Lila Karim

Bio: Lila Karim is a visual artist and printmaker from Istanbul. Her intricate patterns and vibrant designs draw inspiration from Ottoman architecture and Islamic art. With over a decade of experience, Lila’s works seamlessly blend historical motifs with contemporary aesthetics.

Description: Lila's collection, “Whispers of the Past,” features a series of hand-printed works that reimagine traditional designs through modern techniques. Her art celebrates cultural heritage while introducing bold interpretations of symmetry and geometry. Her pieces captivate audiences with their intricate detail and timeless elegance.
